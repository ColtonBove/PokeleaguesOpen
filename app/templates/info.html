About PokeLeagues

Pokeleagues is a web application for online pokemon tournaments, draft leagues, and much more. Pokeleagues facilitates all of the social aspects of hosting tournaments and any kind of pokemon battles, but is meant to be used in conjunction with an official pokemon game, pokemon showdown, or any other type of pokemon simulator. It’s free to anyone, and is meant to allow users to participate or view a variety of different tournaments and battles.
